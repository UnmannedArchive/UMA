About

Seethrough was born from a simple question: what if our clothes could be as honest as we aspire to be?

We craft garments that play with visibility—where fabric weight, cut, and silhouette create moments of revelation. Every piece is designed to be worn alone or layered, each combination revealing something new.

Sustainable materials. Transparent supply chains. Clothing that lets you see through to what matters.
